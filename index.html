Why Make a Website?

This website is designed to tell a little bit about me. These aren't secrets, but a few basic traits that make me, me!

When I was a little boy, I loved building things. Legos, K'NEX, Lincoln Logs...you name it and I would build with it. I'd use these things to build everything from cars to boats to hospitals. But what I loved the most was rocket ships. From a young age, my dad taught me about space. We were always in awe of the cosmos and learning about what it takes to go there. And from there I developed a love for technology. To grow, to compete, to advance, we get smarter. This is my goal - to learn new skills and paradigms to solve faster, build more, and go further. We have so much more to learn about the world!
