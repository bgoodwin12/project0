Here's a brief listing of my favorite Mexican Restaurants!

Restaurant Rating Comments
With Love Burritos 4/5 Maastricht, NL. Good burritos - for Europe. And pickled cabbage on a burrito is a great add to my repertoire.
Cultivar 5/5 Oklahoma City, OK. Simply the best queso and margs I've ever had. Hands down. Good tacos too.
Guadalajara 3/5 Charlottesville, VA. We call this "dirty" Mexican. It's everything you know and love about Tex-Mex without any frills or expectations of frills.

I love mexican food! It makes me feel terrible afterwards, but I love sitting down and eating too many chips and salsa and then still stuffing down a whole meal. Good mexican food is a real joy for me and I can't wait to have some when I return to the United States.
