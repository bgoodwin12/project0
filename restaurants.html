Here's a brief listing of my favorite Mexican Restaurants!

Restaurant Rating Comments
With Love Burritos 4/5 Good burritos - for Europe. And pickled cabbage on a burrito is a great add to my repertoire.
Cultivar 5/5 Simply the best queso and margs I've ever had. Hands down. Good tacos too.
Guadalajara 3/5 We call this "dirty" Maxican. It's everything you know and love about Tex-Mex without any frills or expectations of frills.
