Hobbies that I love

Here's a short list of my favorite activities in no particular order:

  • Skiing

    I started skiing with my dad at age 6, so is is as much bonding experience as it is incrediblely relaxing and exhilarating.

  • Reading

    Non-fiction especially, including memoirs and histories. As they say: leaders are readers.

  • Travel

    Living in Europe, I love the ease of access so many different cultures. Soaking up other cultures is a beautiful way to spend my time.

  • Ultimate Frisbee

    Just a fantastic exercise. I love getting out there and burning off some energy running around.
